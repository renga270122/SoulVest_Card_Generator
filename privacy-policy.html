Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

SoulVest Card Generator is designed with a privacy-first approach. Most card creation actions happen locally in your browser or app on your device.

Information We Process

  • Card inputs you provide (recipient name, message text, selected options)
  • Optional image uploads used to generate your card
  • Locally stored preferences/drafts in browser storage

How Data Is Used

  • To generate and render greeting cards you request
  • To save drafts/history locally for convenience
  • To support sharing/export features you trigger

Storage and Sharing

By default, card data is stored locally on your device (for draft/history features). We do not require account registration for core usage.

Third-Party Services

If analytics or external integrations are enabled, those providers may process limited usage data based on their own policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear local drafts/history from within the app
  • Avoid optional sharing features if you do not want to transmit content

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact the app publisher through the official app listing or project repository.
